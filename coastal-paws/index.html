What we offer

Full care, from nose to tail

From routine checkups to urgent care, we've got everything your pet needs under one roof — right here in Pacific Beach.

🩺

Wellness Exams

Annual and bi-annual checkups to keep your pet healthy, happy, and up to date on vaccines and preventative care.

🔬

Diagnostics & Lab

On-site bloodwork, urinalysis, and digital X-rays so we can get answers fast without sending you somewhere else.

🦷

Dental Care

Professional cleanings, extractions, and dental health guidance to keep those chompers in top shape.

💉

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's age, breed, and specific San Diego environment risks.

🚑

Urgent Care

Same-day appointments for when something just doesn't seem right. We keep slots open every day for urgent needs.

✂️

Surgery

Soft tissue and orthopedic procedures performed by our experienced surgical team in our fully equipped OR.

About us

A neighborhood clinic with big-city care

We opened Coastal Paws in 2013 with one simple goal: give Pacific Beach pet owners a clinic that felt warm, unhurried, and genuinely invested in their animals — not just their appointment slot.

  • ✓Fear-free certified practice — low-stress handling for anxious pets
  • ✓Same vet, every visit — no rotating staff, real continuity of care
  • ✓Transparent pricing with no surprise fees at checkout
  • ✓Online booking, reminders, and medical records in your pocket
