Our story

California craft meets coastal soul

We opened The Salty Palate in Little Italy with a single conviction: that great food is equal parts ingredient, technique, and intention. Our menu changes with the seasons, sourced from within 100 miles of our kitchen.

Executive Chef Elena Voss brings 15 years of fine dining to a space that feels like home — warm, unhurried, and always surprising.
